About Us

J&P Planning Solutions was founded in 2021 with the mission to turn expert project planning into a strategic asset for clients across industries. The company is led by a planning engineer with over a decade of experience managing complex projects across Europe and South America.

With a strong foundation in engineering and a deep understanding of project controls, our founder has worked on diverse, high-stakes initiatives—from large-scale infrastructure to industrial and energy developments. This experience revealed the transformative impact that effective planning can have on a project's success.

At J&P Planning Solutions, we provide expert consultancy in planning, scheduling, and project controls. We tailor our solutions to each client's unique challenges, using data-driven strategies and industry best practices to ensure projects are delivered on time and with confidence.

We don't just create plans—we build clarity, drive, and results.

Our Team

Meet the experienced professionals behind J&P Planning Solutions

Jorge Moutinho

Founder & Director | PMP®

The Founder and Director of J&P Planning Solutions, established in 2021 to deliver expert consultancy in project planning, scheduling, and project controls. With over 14 years of experience across Europe and South America, he brings deep expertise in managing complex projects across sectors such as infrastructure, energy, manufacturing, and construction.

As a certified Project Management Professional (PMP®) and trained civil engineer, Jorge has led and supported the planning and controls for major capital projects totaling over €10 billion in value. With a strong focus on clarity, structure, and results, project teams have benefited from advanced planning systems, risk-informed strategies, and clear, data-driven reporting tailored to both contractor and client perspectives.

Camila Silva

Senior Project Planner

As a Senior Project Planner with over a decade of experience, Camila is a dedicated and detail-oriented professional specializing in scheduling, project controls, and cost management across large-scale projects. Her background as a Civil Engineer, combined with a Postgraduate Degree in Finance, provides a strong foundation for understanding both the technical and financial dimensions of project delivery.

Throughout her career, she has worked with global industry leaders, where she's successfully developed and managed complex project schedules for data centres, pharmaceutical facilities, commercial and residential construction developments in South America and Europe.

Dalton Ribeiro
